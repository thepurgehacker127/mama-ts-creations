How It All Started

More Than a Business — It's a Calling

Mama T's Creations was born out of a simple belief: that a handmade gift, made with intention and love, can touch a heart in ways that words alone cannot.

What started as a passion for creating beautiful, personalized items for family and friends grew into something much bigger — a small business rooted in faith, fueled by creativity, and built on the joy of seeing people light up when they receive something made just for them.

Every shirt, tumbler, mug, and gift that leaves our hands carries a little piece of our heart. We don't mass produce. We don't cut corners. We take our time because we believe you and the people you love deserve something real.
