Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mama T's Creations ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or place an order, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you fill out our Custom Order form or Contact form, we collect information you voluntarily provide, which may include:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Order details (product type, design preferences, text, images you describe)
  • Shipping address
  • Payment preference (we do not collect or store full payment card numbers on our website)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process and fulfill your custom order
  • Communicate with you about your order, including design previews and shipping updates
  • Respond to questions submitted through our Contact form
  • Improve our products and customer experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

How Your Information Is Submitted & Stored

Our order and contact forms are processed through a third-party form service (Formspree), which securely transmits your submission to our email inbox. We do not maintain a separate customer database on our website — submissions live in our email and any order-tracking tools we use internally.

Payment Information

Payments are handled directly through third-party payment processors (such as PayPal, Cash App, Zelle, or a card processor) once your design is approved. We do not store your full payment details on our website.

Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are most popular). This information is used only to improve the site and is not used to personally identify you.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate parts of our website, including our form processor and font/icon providers (Google Fonts, Font Awesome). These services may collect limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of standard web functionality, governed by their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we have on file related to your order, or to request that we delete information that is no longer needed for an active or past order, where reasonably possible.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults placing orders. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out through our Contact page.
